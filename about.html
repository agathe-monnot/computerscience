What is the Curriculum for Excellence?

This website is following the curriculum set out by Education Scotland. Curriculum for Excellence is a program currently used within Scottish Education with the primary goal of providing a coherent, more flexible and enriched curriculum. It is intended to help children and young people to gain knowledge, skills and attributes needed for living in the 21st century, including skills for learning, life and work.

More about Education Scotland

Why computer science?

In a world where computers and electronic devices are at the center of our lives (for leisure or work), computer science is an essential topic for children to learn so they can use these techologies to study, to play games and to prepare themselves for the future.
It is also essential that they learn the importance of omline safety, therefore this website emphasises on how to stay vigilant on the internet and encourage children to communicate with adults when they do research or watch content online.

It is important to help and support your child through their learning journey. This website is a great tool to learn and play together and for you to stay on top of what's been taught in class.
